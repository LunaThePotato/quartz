### House Scorn-Exemplar
**Key Figures**  
1. **Director Vexis Thorn**  
   - **Role**: CEO of *Thorn Analytical Systems*, a biotech firm pioneering genetic transmutation and psychotropic drugs.  
   - **Personality**: Coldly logical, views ethics as “inefficient.” Wears a lab coat lined with silver runes.  
   - **Secret**: She’s dying of blight poisoning and experiments on herself to cheat death.  
   - **Underlings**:  
     - **Dr. Kael Marrow** (Bio-Alchemist): Develops hybrid creatures (e.g., blight-infused Warforged). Secretly sells prototypes to Seccna.  
     - **Serra Vind** (Data Overlord): A cyborg who monitors Laborers via neural implants. Owns *Cognate Networks*, a surveillance firm.

**Associated Companies**  
- **Cognate Networks**: Data brokerage selling Laborer biometrics to the highest bidder.  
- **Marrow Pharmaceuticals**: Produces stimulants for Laborers… with addictive side effects.  
- **Blightroot AgriCorp**: Grows genetically modified crops in blightstorms.
