#### **Stoneshriek Hollow** (Mining Town)

- **Location**: A cavernous valley rich in arcanite veins.
    
- **Key Features**:
    
    - **The Echoing Mines**: Tunnels where pickaxes sing ancient runic hymns.
        
    - **The Gemcutters’ Guild**: Crafts runestones for Thalia’s military.
        
- **Key Figures**:
    
    - **Foreman Durak Stonefist** (Dwarf, Male): A traditionalist who bans women from the mines.
        
    - **Talia Embervein** (Kobold, Female): A genius prospector ostracized for her race.
