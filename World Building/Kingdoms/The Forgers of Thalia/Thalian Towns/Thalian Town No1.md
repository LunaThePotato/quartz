####  **Port Frostreach** (Northern Port Town)

- **Location**: A frozen harbor on the Northern Sea, accessible only via icebreaker ships.
    
- **Key Features**:
    
    - **The Frostwharf**: Docks lined with enchanted braziers melting ice for trade ships.
        
    - **The Salted Kraken Inn**: A raucous tavern frequented by Guild of the Seas corsairs.
        
- **Key Figures**:
    
    - **Captain Eldrin Frostbeard** (Dwarf, Male): Grizzled leader of the icebreaker fleet.
        
    - **Lira “Iceveil”** (Half-Orc, Female): A smuggler running blight-resistant crops to displaced villages.
