The current year the Campaign takes place in is:
Year 256 A.Q. (After Quiescence)

Year: 76 A.Q. - Marshal is born in the Empire of Seccna.

Year 78 A.Q - (Marshall is 2 yrs old) Marshall is taken away from his Debt-Ridden Family by Debt-Collectors.

Year 89 A.Q - (Marshall is 13 years old)
***Nightingale*** becomes Friends with an young Drow boy named ***Crow*** and a Young High-Elven GIrl named ***Raven***. The three are set to be trained as Child soldiers and are expected to be auctioned off by the Kidnappers at an appropriate time.

Year 140 A.Q: (Marshall is 64 years old)
***Nightingale*** & ***Raven** wakes up to see that their friend Crow is nowhere to be seen but a letter left behind him. Shortly there after an opening to escape their Prison was presented to them, to which after they escaped to a safe environment in the forest. They both open the letter to see that the cause of their escape was provided by Crow and that he's now Presumed dead from there on. They Continue on their Escape from Seccna onto Alcheim Territory to make their way to a promising future in Lisseren Over the course of a few months.

Year 140 A.Q - 176 A.Q: (Marshall is now 100 Years old by 176 A.Q)
Nightingale & Raven Spend their lives together in Lisseren under new Names ***Tharivol*** (Nightingale) & ***Charlotte*** (Raven) as a married couple. Living a peaceful life as best as they could. Within those years, Tharivol took up on Bardic Path under the College of Swords & Charlotte as a working woman(?)>
> - It was all peace till He found his Beloved Charlotte Slain by their old Companion Crow who's gone mad and came after the two.
> - After Slaying his Best friend Crow, Tharivol set out to seek out his original Family that he was taken away from. From his Family, he learned that his Real name was ***Marshall Rose***.
> - After Reuniting with his Family, Marshall set off on a journey in hops to better himself. That's till he met an Human Woman (Let's Call her ***Maria*** for now) Who was sent off to assassinate him by the Organization that Robbed him of his family.
> - Eventually Maria decided to not Kill off Marshall after seeing his Resolve & Instead opted to team up with him to Disrupt n Dismantle the Organization of Elves that Were after him.

Year 176 A.Q - 236 A.Q: (Marshall is now 160 by 236 A.Q)
**Marshall** then set off to the countryside of Lisseren in his early years & established an Orphanage, taking in lost children without a home/relatives from nearby Towns & become the **Father Figure** he couldn't be with the loss of his Beloved Wife Charlotte & their Unborn Child.
> - **Maria** would occasionally drop by the orphanage to drop off kids she liberated from the Elven Organization.
> - **Marshall** also took up helping out plans to liberate more children from the Organization with Maria.
> - Overtime, the Orphanage was growing to a capacity where Maria enlisted the help of an High-Elf Maiden (Who we'll call for now **Olivia**) who became the Mother Figure for the Orphaned Children. *Marshal Keeping himself reserved from* **Olivia**.
> -After years of service, in 212 A.Q., **Maria** would pass away at the old age of 73 and **Olivia** would then take her place within the Orphanage. *Humans unfortunately don't live too long...*

Year 236 A.Q - 256 A.Q [Present Time]: (Marshall would be 180 by 256 A.Q)
Marshall would see Kids come and go at the Orphanage, living far from the life of violence he once lived in many years ago. That was till he decided to take on a Pilgrimage once more with his Sword & Bardic Wits to seek any more lost children throughout the land to send off to his orphanage...
> - To which we finally arrive at the present day where we see our fellow Bard enjoying a mug of Ale... One he took too many of to which he found himself in the confines of a jail cell... *in the Nude?* How did our fellow Bard get here in the first place? *Who knows...*
