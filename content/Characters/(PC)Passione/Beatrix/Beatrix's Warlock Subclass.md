### **Mistress of Death**
 
 [[Beatrix|Beatrix's]] patron has spent their unlife gaining control over death itself. They can manipulate and control death itself. To your patron, death is only a state of being that can easily be altered to their whims. Your patron may be a deity over death, an elder lich or a necromancer that has surpassed their mortal limits.

### ***Warden of Death***

*Starting at 1st level, You're able trap an defeated undead creature (That has been weakened not killed) into your Soul Lantern. Upon capture, the undead creature will forcibly be bent to your will if their CR score meets your current Warlock Level or below [Or at DM's Discretion]. You'll be able to detain a number of undead creatures equal to your Current Proficiency Bonus. [Total of: 2]* 
>`If the undead creature is reluctant (Over your current warlock lv or at DM's Discretion) to submit to your will. You can still opt to detain the creature into your Soul Lantern without taking any space. However, upon the start of the next day, the captured undead may choose to attempt to break out of it's containment to which you both would have to contest a roll of a 1d20+(Prof. modifier). On a pass, the detained creature will continue on being held prisoner but will gain an additional attempt of escape every 3 days of containment. On a Fail, the detained undead will escape the Lantern and can't be detained again furthermore`

*You can summon a captured undead as an Bonus Action and they'll appear in a unoccupied space of your choosing within 15ft of you. Within that casting time, you can mentally issue an command to the creature (Also if they're within 120 feet of you). You decide what action the creature will take and where it will move during its next turn, or you can issue a general command, such as to guard a particular chamber or corridor. If you issue no commands, the creature only defends itself against hostile creatures. Once given an order, the creature continues to follow it until its task is complete.*

*You can also recall the Undead back into your Lantern as an Bonus Action and they'll retain the HP and spell slots available (If applicable) until a long rest. If the Undead has been slain on the Field, it will be unable to return back into the Lantern.*

#### {***Superior Undead***}
At 6th level, undead under control gain the following benefits:

> ● The creatures hit point maximum is increased by an amount equal to your warlock level.
> ● The creatures adds half your proficiency bonus (rounded up) to its armor class, attack rolls and damage rolls while you are within 90 feet of it.

#### {***Dominion over Mortality***}
At 10th level, You gain resistance to Necrotic Damage & are granted an additional spell slot alongside with the ability to cast {[[Homebrew Spells#***Malignant Rage*** (*Spell Lv. 4 Transmutation*)|Malignant Rage]]} as a ***Reaction*** once per Short or Long Rest. 

#### {***Flesh of my Flesh***}
At 14th level, your Patron's Powers Have a profound effect on your body. You no longer need to Eat, Drink or Breathe. Upon losing a limb, you can use your action to reattach any of your severed limbs, and your maximum Hit Points can’t be reduced.

### {**Additional Eldritch Invocations options**}

(***Eldritch Invocation: Dominate Dead***) - *Prerequisites: 3rd level*

As an action, you target one undead you can see within 30 ft and invoke your patron’s authority over it. It must make a Charisma saving throw. On a failed save, it must obey your commands for the next 24 hours, or until you use this Invocation again. Undead who's CR are equal to or greater than your Warlock level are immune to this Invocation. Once you use this invocation, you cannot do so again until you finish a short or long rest.

(***Eldritch Invocation: The Walking Dead***) - *Prerequisite: 5th Level, Pact of the Chain*

You can cast the Animate Dead spell once targeting a single creature without expending a warlock spell slot and bind that creature as your familiar as described in the find familiar spell. It keeps the stats as described by the animate dead spell and can still attack as normal. For a creature to be eligible to be bound as your familiar it's CR must be lower than 1/2 your proficiency bonus rounded up.

(***Eldritch Invocation: Greater Creator***) - *Prerequisites: 11th level*

You can cast Create Undead by expending one Mystic Arcanum spell you have. The spell is considered to be cast at the same level as that Mystic Arcanum.
