
**Full Name**: _Ransu Enmamaru_ (ランス・閻魔丸) (Old Last Name: Homurashi (Flame Master))

- **Meaning**: “Lance of the Enma Clan’s Flame.”
    
- **Storybeat**: His fire magic manifests at age 15 during a raid, symbolizing his coming-of-age as a defender.

1. **Meaning**: Combines _Enma_ (閻魔, the Japanese Buddhist king of hell who judges souls) with _-maru_ (丸, a suffix often used in historical samurai names, symbolizing nobility or completeness).
    
    - **Symbolism**: Suggests a lineage tied to hell’s justice, but tempered by honor. Lance could inherit a role as a "judge" of threats to his town, wielding both mercy and infernal power.
        
    - **Duality**: Perfect for a kind soul with a demonic lineage — he’s not a villain, but a guardian who understands darkness intimately.
2. **Nickname Potential**:
    
    - Friends/family might call him **“Maru”** (丸) as a term of endearment, which means “circle” (symbolizing unity or wholeness). This contrasts beautifully with his hellish roots, hinting at his journey to reconcile both sides.
        
3. **Flow with "Lance"**:
    
    - **Lance Enmamaru** has a rhythmic, almost poetic sound, blending Western and Japanese aesthetics. It feels heroic but grounded.

1. **Backstory Hooks**:
    
    - His clan could have once served Enma, tasked with punishing evil souls. Now, they seek redemption by defending the living.
        
    - Lance’s signature weapon (nodachi, naginata) might be inscribed with infernal runes that glow when he judges a foe’s intent.

Shares A deep Companionship with [[Maiko (Aki)]] since their youth.
