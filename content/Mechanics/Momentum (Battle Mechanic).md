### **Momentum**

**Momentum** is a short-term resource that rewards mobile, combo style play. Players can collect Momentum Stacks and spend them to add for damage to Weapon Attacks or use a Secondary (*or even Empowered*) effect of their Art, however this mechanic may not apply to every Art.

### **Momentum Stack Management**

 A character's **Maximum Momentum stack** is equal to Half (*Round Up*) of their **Current Proficiency Bonus**. They can gain 1 Momentum stack per round (*up to your maximum*) when you do the following:

- Taking a Dashing Action.
- Casting a Spell or Art that spins you in a Circular Motion.
- Completing the sequence: *Attack Action(1)>Bonus Action Art>Attack Action(2)* on the same turn.

(**Duration & Retention**) A Momentum Stack lasts for 1 round (*until the start of your next turn*). Each time you take an Attack action, all existing Momentum stacks on you refresh for another round. You lose all Momentum Stacks immediately if you are **Knocked Unconscious**, become subject to a movement inhibiting Condition (Such as [[Status Conditions (Crowd Control {CC})#**Incapacitated**|Incapacitated]]), and at the end of Combat.

(**Spending Momentum**) When you land a hit with a Melee Attack, you may spend a single Momentum Stack to add a bonus damage die to that attack. It'll still be expended if the attack misses. The bonus die scales by character level:

| Character Level  | 1-4 | 5-8 | 9-12 | 13-16 | 17-20 |
| ---------------- | --- | --- | ---- | ----- | ----- |
| Bonus Damage Die | 1d4 | 1d6 | 1d8  | 1d10  | 1d12  |
The bonus die uses the same damage type as the attack's **primary damage die**.
(**Example**:) A Shortsword (*1d6 Slashing*) that spends one Momentum Stack deals 1d6+1d4 Slashing damage on that hit (*plus your normal modifiers*).

### **Bonus Momentum Stacks**

Although the general maximum Momentum Stacks a Character generate is limited, there are certain **Classes** that can increase a Character's maximum Momentum stacks. If you qualify, you gain additional stacks equal to half (Round Up) of your **Dexterity Modifier**.

(**Classes that gain Bonus Momentum**:) Bard, Monk, Ranger, Rouge, Fighter (Champion & Battle Master)

Characters in other Classes may still gain the Bonus Momentum benefit by taking Mobility Based Feats (Limit 2 feats; taking more than two grants no further Bonus).
