Union Combos is a Battle Mechanic inspired by _[Xenoblade Chronicles 2](https://xenoblade.fandom.com/wiki/Xenoblade_Chronicles_2 "Xenoblade Chronicles 2")_, _[Torna ~ The Golden Country](https://xenoblade.fandom.com/wiki/Xenoblade_Chronicles_2:_Torna_~_The_Golden_Country "Xenoblade Chronicles 2: Torna ~ The Golden Country")_ (*Driver Combos*), and _[Xenoblade Chronicles 3](https://www.xenoserieswiki.org/wiki/Xenoblade_Chronicles_3 "Xenoblade Chronicles 3")_ (*Art Combos*) brought to life in the world of Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition (*2014 & 2024*). They consist of [[Weapon Arts|Weapon Arts]] performed in a particular sequence. A Union Combo begins when an Art inflicts :FighterArts_break: [[Status Conditions (Crowd Control {CC})#**Break**|Break]] on the enemy, after which it can be :FighterArts_topple: [[Status Conditions (Crowd Control {CC})#**Prone**|Toppled]], then :FighterArts_launch: [[Status Conditions (Crowd Control {CC})#**Knock-up**|Launched]] and finally either :Conditions_burst: [[Status Conditions (Crowd Control {CC})#**Burst**|Bursted]] or :FighterArts_smash: [[Status Conditions (Crowd Control {CC})#**Knockdown**|Smashed]] by using Arts or Spells with those effects before the enemy recovers.

| **Stage 3**: Launch    | :FighterArts_launch: | **Effect time**: Till the end of the Target's next turn.<br><br>_*If the Target has it's :FighterArts_topple: **Topple** condition consumed by an :FighterArts_launch: **Launch** afflicted ability, deal an extra 2d6 Force Damage on-hit upon inflicting :FighterArts_launch: **Launch**._                                                                                                                            |
