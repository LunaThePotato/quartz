**Weapon Arts** are special techniques that the Player can use with a variety of effects with their **Signature Blade** or **Innate Abilities**. While most deal damage, some allow the user to either :FighterArts_defense: [[Health Shields (Homebrew)|Shield]] or :FighterArts_heal: **Heal** themselves, an close ally, the party. Some Arts are stronger against a certain type of enemy, and some become more effective if an enemy is hit from a certain direction.

Arts with a **special effect** are indicated by a smaller symbol in the lower half of the icon:

|           Icon            | Description                                                            |
| :-----------------------: | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
|  :WeaponArts_art_front:   | Extra Damage if attacking from the enemy's front.                      |
|   :WeaponArts_art_rear:   | Extra Damage if attacking from the enemy's back.                       |
|   :WeaponArts_art_side:   | Extra Damage if attacking from the enemy's side.                       |
|  :WeaponArts_art_cancel:  | Increases Damage ratio after canceling an Weapon-Attack.               |
| :WeaponArts_art_species:  | Does extra damage to enemies of a certain type (e.g. aquatic enemies). |
| :WeaponArts_art_recharge: | Refund the Charges used for an Art.                                    |
- There are at least 4 Weapon Art Slots Active at a time on a **Signature Blade**.
- Players can swap out Arts in their pool when outside of Combat.
- More Arts can be acquired or learned throughout the campaign. 
- Players are able to hold each other's signature blades but can't use it to its full potential. (Case by Case Basis)

-# *I would like to **Emphasize** on this that I've never intended to have these **Signature Blades** to be shared around from player to player... it's an interesting idea to think on in the future but for now these Weapons are strictly tied to it's intended user.*
