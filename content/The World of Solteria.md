[**Before Quiescence**] (B.Q.) was an Era of Chaos where the old gods that once kept order within the Mortal Plane faded n passed on... leaving the Old world in ruin. Kingdoms have come and gone in this Era...

[**Quiescence**] (Q.) is the Era where the Continents where just mere isolated communities unaware of eachother's existence. 

[**After Quiescence**] (A.Q.) The Continents of Lisseren, Alcheim, Thalia, Seccna, Forsworn, & The Guild of the Seas Emerged from Quiescence & eventually Discovered eachother's Exisistence.
