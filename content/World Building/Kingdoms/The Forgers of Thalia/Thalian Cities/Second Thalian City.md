#### **Emberhearth Haven** (Miss Martha’s Domain)

- **Location**: A volcanic plateau in the southern Stoneveils, warmed by geothermal vents.
    
- **Key Features**:
    
    - **The Gilded Veil**: Miss Martha’s opulent matchmaking salon, filled with enchanted mirrors that reflect desires.
        
    - **Ashen Bazaar**: A black market for forbidden romances, demonic trinkets, and smuggled Seccnian wine.
        
    - **Velamora’s Spire**: A hidden temple where Martha communes with her patron.
        
- **Key Figures**:
    
    - **Miss Martha** (Tiefling, Male presenting as Female): The devil’s matchmaker.
        
    - **Garrick Emberhold** (Human, Male): A merchant prince who funds Martha’s schemes in exchange for political marriages.
        
    - **Sorrel Scaleshadow** (Dragonborn, Female): A disgraced scholar documenting Thalia’s gender inequalities.
