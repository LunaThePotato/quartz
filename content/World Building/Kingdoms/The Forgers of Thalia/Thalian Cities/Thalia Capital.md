#### **Stoneveil Citadel** 
- **Location**: Highest peak of the Stoneveil Mountains, surrounded by glaciers and treacherous passes.
    
- **Key Features**:
    
    - **The Iron Spire**: Lord Olden’s fortress, carved into the mountain itself. Its walls are etched with ancient runes that repel invaders.
        
    - **The Hall of Ancestors**: A cathedral-like archive storing Thalia’s history in rune-inscribed stone tablets.
        
    - **Frostforged Market**: A bustling bazaar heated by magma runes, trading everything from arcanite ore to enchanted ale.
        
- **Key Figures**:
    
    - **Lord Olden**: The stoic dwarven king, rarely seen outside the Iron Spire.
        
    - **Captain Brynja Ironhelm** (Dwarf, Female): Commander of the Forge Knights. Secretly trains women in combat.
        
    - **High Priestess Thalara** (Dwarf, Female): Traditionalist leader of the Ancestor Cult. Opposes Miss Martha’s influence.
