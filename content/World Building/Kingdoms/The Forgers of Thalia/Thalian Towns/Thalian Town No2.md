#### **Ironmaw Anchorage** (Southern Port Town)

- **Location**: A fortified harbor on the warmer southern coast, facing Seccna’s waters.
    
- **Key Features**:
    
    - **The Ironmaw Shipyards**: Produces rune-insulated warships to deter Seccnian raids.
        
    - **The Tideborn Arena**: A combat pit where sailors and Seccnian prisoners fight for coin.
        
- **Key Figures**:
    
    - **Dockmaster Grondar** (Minotaur, Male): Imposes brutal order on the shipyards.
        
    - **Marisol Voss** (Human, Female): A spy posing as a fishmonger, feeding intel to Lisseren.
