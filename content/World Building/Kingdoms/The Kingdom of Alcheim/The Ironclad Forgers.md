### House 
**Chief Engineer Torvin Rook**  
   - **Role**: CEO of *Rook Foundry Dynamics*, the largest Warforged manufacturer in Solteria.  
   - **Personality**: A brash, grease-stained genius who speaks in mechanical metaphors. Missing an arm replaced with a magitech prosthetic.  
   - **Secret**: He’s building a secret AI to overthrow Prince Zein-Glassc.  
   - **Underlings**:  
     - **Hessia Grind** (Factory Overseer): Runs Union Isles labor camps. Brutal but secretly funnels profits to Laborer rebellions.  
     - **Zev Hex** (Tech Savant): A hacker who sabotages rival magitech. Owns *Hexwire Solutions*, a black-market mod shop.

**Associated Companies**  
- **Hexwire Solutions**: Sells illegal magitech mods (e.g., phase-cloak disruptors).  
- **Gearspark Utilities**: Maintains Alcheim’s infrastructure… and plants backdoors for corporate espionage.  
- **Ironroot Fabrications**: Produces biomechanical forests for terraforming blightstorms.
