### House Vaelzeth-Arcanum

**Key Figures**  
1. **High Arcanist Malara Voss**  
   - **Role**: CEO of *Voss Enigma Industries*, a conglomerate specializing in interdimensional magic and Vastayan essence extraction.  
   - **Personality**: Charismatic but ruthless, with a silver tongue and a penchant for binding contracts written in blood.  
   - **Secret**: She’s secretly a half-Vastayan hybrid, using her lineage to stabilize unstable magic.  
   - **Underlings**:  
     - **Lyra Keth** (Head Researcher): Obsessed with replicating Ionia’s magical weave. Conducts illegal experiments on Wood Elves.  
     - **Dranek Voidwalker** (Enforcer): A shadow sorcerer who “disappears” rivals. Owns a stake in *Eclipse Security*, a mercenary guild.

**Associated Companies**  
- **Eclipse Security**: Mercenary guild specializing in magical espionage.  
- **Voidspire Logistics**: Smuggles forbidden artifacts via pocket dimensions.  
- **Aetherflow Essence**: Harvests Vastayan essence for magitech fuel.
