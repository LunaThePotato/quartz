You can use Healing Potions once as a ***Free Action*** per turn. If a Player or Character wants to consume a secondary Healing Potion in the same turn, it'll cost a ***Bonus Action*** instead. Any other Potions of all kinds would still require to take a ***Bonus Action*** to consume whilst in combat.

- {**Potion of Healing**} - It's 2d6+4 
- {**Potion of Greater Healing**} - It's 4d6+6
- {**Potion of Superior Healing**} - It's 8d6+8
- {**Potion of Supreme Healing**} - Heals for 10d6+10

Throwing a Potion of any kind to an Creature will apply it's effects upon hitting them directly dealing usually 1d4 bludgeoning damage depending the material of the Potion Container.
