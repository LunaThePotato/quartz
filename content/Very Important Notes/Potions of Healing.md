You can use Healing Potions once as a ***Free Action*** per turn. For other potions they'll use a *Bonus Action* instead, including if the character wants to consume a second Healing Potion on the same turn.

- {**Potion of Healing**} - It's 2d6+4 
- {**Potion of Greater Healing**} - It's 4d6+6
- {**Potion of Superior Healing**} - It's 8d6+8
- {**Potion of Supreme Healing**} - Heals for 10d6+10
