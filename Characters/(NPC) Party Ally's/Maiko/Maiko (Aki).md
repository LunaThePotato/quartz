Real name: Ayaki Michiko

### Ayaki

- **Note:** The meaning of “Ayaki” can vary because it isn’t as common, and its meaning depends on the kanji used.
- **Possible Kanji Example:**
    - **綾木:**
        - 綾 (aya): “design,” “pattern,” or “brocade”
        - 木 (ki): “tree”
    - **Interpretation:** This could be read as “patterned tree” or “brocade tree.”
- **Meaning:** “An uncommon figure,” reflecting a tradition of valuing individuality and exceptional qualities. It was given to those who stood out for their extraordinary skills or traits, and today it continues to symbolize personal distinction and uniqueness.
### Michiko

- **Common Kanji:** 美智子
- **Meaning:**
    - 美 (mi): “beauty”
    - 智 (chi): “wisdom”
    - 子 (ko): “child”
- **Interpretation:** “Child of beautiful wisdom” or “beautiful, wise child.”
