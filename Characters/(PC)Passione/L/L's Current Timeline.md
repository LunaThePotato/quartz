[**Before Quiescence**] (B.Q.) was an Era of Chaos where the old gods that once kept order within the Mortal Plane faded n passed on... leaving the Old world in ruin. Kingdoms have come and gone in this Era...

[**Quiescence**] (Q.) is the Era where the Continents where just mere isolated communities unaware of eachother's existence. 

[**After Quiescence**] (A.Q.) The Continents of Lisseren, Alcheim, Thalia, Seccna, Forsworn, & The Guild of the Seas Emerged from Quiescence & eventually Discovered eachother's Exisistence.

- The current year the Campaign takes place in is: `Year 256 A.Q.` (After Quiescence)

`Year 213 A.Q. - 227 A.Q.:` - (L would be 13 years old in this period but turning 14 in a few days after the destruction of the orphanage by 227 A.Q.)
L was Born in the Empire of Seccna where he would be raised in an Orphanage owned by ***Drake*** and his ***Aasimar High-Elf Wife*** who they soon adopted L as their son. Drake taking L under his wing as a man of Science and L being showered with love by his ***Aasimar Mother***. As L grew up in the Church, he was despised & hated by the children & Clerics of the Orphanage. Children bullied L for a Devil being Showered in love n affection by the Orphanage owners as the Clerics would look away at the misdeeds of the Children towards L for his Demonic Origin.
> - L's Aasimar Mother was really Protective and loved him with all her heart...
> - L for the first time learns about Vastayans from Drake's old hand book he had during his pirate days.
> - L would also reconcile with one of his bullies and befriend them, a young Satyr girl named ***Alex*** while also meeting a Female Half-Orc Teacher named ***Orianna***.
> - Around this time, A Loxidon would come by when L was Alone. Sparking a flame within L, to retaliate... to fight back! As the Gods don't care for us mortals...
> - One day (in 225 A.Q.), Drake was called to go a year long overseas for Business and promised to be back for L soon. With Drake's Absence and the Children's growing distaste for L, L's Mother decided to take matters into her own hands... To Attack the Children who Bullied L in Secret behind Closed doors. L's Mother's love deepened to a point where it turned to *lust* as she then Forced herself onto L. throughout the time of Drake's absence...
> - **Alex** & **Orianna** would also depart from the Orphanage too shortly there after.
> - (In 226 A.Q.) L eventually grew to think Drake was just as bad as her to which L would turn to take his own life till the same Loxidon intervened to use his rage to destroy the Church and to take his revenge against his own mother.
> - After a week of preparation, L Tortured his Aasimar Mother to near death & set the Church Ablaze with the Children, Clerics, & Mother in it down to the ground.
> - L leaves the Decimated grounds of the Orphanage with the Loxidon... And for Drake to return moments later mourning for not the loss of his church but his Son in the flames...

`Year 227 A.Q. - 254 A.Q.:` - (L would be 41 Years old by 254 A.Q.)
After burning down the Orphanage, L is taken under the Loxidon's Wing to work at the Syndicate. There he would meet a Female Wood-Elf named Shasa, A *Creature* named Mask, & a Female Harpy Eagle Vastayan Claire. Together, the 4 were set to take on Tasks from the Elephant and cause havoc throughout the Empire of Seccna.
> - Throughout L's time here, he was slowly coming out of his shell of guilt and sorrow for the destruction of Drake's life's work with the assistance of the 3 stooges.
> - Eventually L regained a few old aspects of himself after a climactic moment in his life where he finally found himself a home where he felt he belong. 
> - Within this time period, L would go on in frequent Sexcapades with Shasa as her passion for him grew. 
> - With L regaining his character, something sparked within him... the chaos... the bloodlust.. the art of combat... the rush of emotions in near death experiences... it all empowered an sadistic urge to kill within him in the heat of combat.
> - This Urge to kill becomes a part of his ideology & will to live... "The Strong Survive and eat the weak, the weak live by appealing to the Strong."
> - L's interest in the Vastayan race grew more with the coming new Law of the Detainment of the Vastayan Race in `Year 244` (L would be 31 at this time)
> - Shortly ther after the Elephant sets the 3 stooges, L, Shasa, Mask away on their own personal quest apart from eachother. L's quest was a search of true love.
> - Within `Year 249 A.Q. - 254 A.Q.` (L would be 36-41 years old in this time span) Mario Pull's L Aside to embark on a lead Mario got of Val'Hazak's Whereabouts in the Underdark, within this time L Spends time with the wizard as he also helps L better his craft in their time there too. However it was all to no avail as Val'Hazak has eluded the duo once more.
> - With the monster Eluding L, An Epiphany shudders over to L. With his reputation scaring off potential opponents and Monsters being too weak... the worst this world can bestow upon him is... Boredom... Boredom... it sounds unfamiliar... every avenue L takes to escape his boredom it just keeps on creeping onto him, *Killing his very being!!!!*  Taking on Jobs around the continent one after another, killing one monster one after another, building up his bounty more and more... *Its just* ***NOT ENOUGH***.... Boredom... it sounds Painful...
> - Eventually L takes his eyes to Queen Marsys as she addresses the public... Boredom... it sounds Familiar... Its all over her eyes, you can feel her stark empty gaze just piercing through your boredom... *maybe a visit with the missus would be a good idea... let's see if anything interesting would brew between us*
> - After a Quick Audience with Queen Marsys, both truck a secretive deal with each other... If L offers up the Vastayan in question to Queen Marsys, she'll offer L any favor she can grant within her power or the sadistic route one of their Free will's, would be up for grabs as L must bring over the most powerful Vastayan he can & see who will come out on top!

`Year 254 A.Q. - 255 A.Q.:` (L would be 42 by 255)

After Dropping Maiko at the Port and parting ways, L goes out his way to seek an audience with the Prince of Alcheim, Princ Zeiglasc.
