### **Weapon Arts**

{**Passive**} - ***The Maiden's Divine Will*** - With the Divine Gifts she inherited, Peach seizes the Battlefield to Slice though enemies of the Faith whilst aiding her Companions. **The Maiden's Divine Will** Grants Peach {**16 Charges**} to expend into her Weapon Arts. She can regain 1d4+1 Charges for every **Takedown** (*Kills and Assist*) on an enemy. She can also replenish 2d4+1 on a **Short Rest** or full upon completing a **Long Rest**.

### **Scythe Waltz**

- **Cost**: 4 Charges
- **Type**: Action
- **Range**: Self (Dash up to 10ft)
- **Effect**: Peach dashes up to 10ft. in a straight line. You make a Melee Weapon Attack against each Creature you move though. On-Hit, Each creature takes 1d8(Slashing)+(Dex)+1d6(:FighterArts_light_element: [Radiant](https://solteria.org/Mechanics/Elemental-Combo-System#_fighterarts_light_element_-light-combos)) Damage.
  > (**Landing Burst**) - When you finish this movement in an unoccupied space, all creatures within 5ft. of you must succeed a DC:14 Dexterity Saving Throw or take 3d8(Slashing)+(Dex)+3d4(:FighterArts_light_element: [Radiant](https://solteria.org/Mechanics/Elemental-Combo-System#_fighterarts_light_element_-light-combos)) Damage. Half Damage on a Pass. Peach can then make one Melee Weapon attack against a creature within 5ft. of her final position. 

-# (**Roaring Skies**) - *If there are any Enemies afflicted with Break, Peach can expend an additional 2 Charges for the **Landing Burst** to also inherit the Launching properties of "**Blade's Reach**" to the enemies within 5ft of her.*

-# (**Obstacle Phasing**) - *You may Dash through a breakable obstacle no more than 5 ft. thick (such as a ruined wall or boulder) as though it were an empty space; if no space is available beyond it, your movement ends at the obstacle’s edge.*

### **Blade's Reach**

- **Cost**: 2 Charges
- **Type**: Bonus Action
- **Range**: 10ft
- **Effect**: Peach swings her Scythe in a targeted direction of 10ft. Enemies within range must succeed a DC: 14 Constitution Saving Throw or take 1d12(*Slashing*)+(*Dex*)+2d4(:FighterArts_light_element: [Radiant](https://solteria.org/Mechanics/Elemental-Combo-System#_fighterarts_light_element_-light-combos)) Damage & be :FighterArts_launch: [[Status Conditions (Crowd Control {CC})#**Knock-up**|Launched]] 10ft into the Air till the end of their 2nd Turn.

### **Fey Wilds Veil**

- **Cost**: 4 Charges
- **Type**: Reaction
- **Range**: Self
- **Duration**: Till the End of her next Turn
- **Effect**: Peach envelopes herself in a Fey Mist. For the duration of the Art- Peach becomes [[Status Conditions (Crowd Control {CC})#**Ghosting**|Ghosted]], gains an additional 10ft of movement speed, and can reduce 1d8+(*Prof.Mod*) of Physical Damage.
  > Peach can also envelop her Allies in the Fey mist upon phasing through them, allowing them to also inherit the effects of this Art.
