*Shields are an addition of hit-points that absorb damage in place of actual Health. They may persist on the recipient to absorb a set amount of damage (Shield Strength) and/or until the duration runs out (Shield Duration). When shielded from multiple sources, damage taken is mitigated by the shield that will expire.*

### Shield Types

- {♦️ **Normal Shields**} - *They absorb all types of damage either it be: Physical, Magic, & True DMG. (Does not protect from Mental/Emotional Dmg) Can be referred as Grey Health.*
- {🔷 **Magic Shields**} - *They only absorb Magic Damage. Can be referred as Blue Health.*
- {🔶 **Physical Shields**} - *They only absorb Physical Damage. Can be referred as Orange Health.*
